How we met

Sarah and I met the summer before we entered 9th grade. Band camp was what truly brought us together. We were pretty quickly friends and spent that entire year building our friendship (and flirting the best way 14 year olds know how). I even had to clean her locker out for her at the end of the year. I’ve been picking up after her ever since!

After a couple of awkward attempts (according to her), I asked her out on August 24th 2010. I don’t think anyone would have suspected that we’d be getting married almost nine years later, but here we are! We have been through everything together and have continued to grow with each other. From getting our driver licenses to going to college, from our dorm rooms being 1000 feet apart to living across the country from each other, we have always chosen to love each other.

We are excited to start this new journey with each other, and we hope that you will be there to celebrate it with us.
